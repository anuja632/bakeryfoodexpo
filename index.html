About

We bring people and ideas together to inspire creativity and growth.

Empower Your Bakery Business with Innovation & Growth

Bakery & Food Tec Expo 2026 is India’s premier event for the bakery, confectionery, and food processing sectors — uniting industry leaders, innovators, and entrepreneurs on one dynamic platform.

Happening from March 13–15, 2026 at the Hills Exhibition Center, Hosur, Tamil Nadu, the expo will showcase cutting-edge technologies, live demos, and global networking opportunities shaping the future of food innovation.

Join us to explore new trends, build partnerships, and experience how innovation and sustainability are transforming India’s bakery and food industry.
